# Interview topic

## Question

Ask a clear platform engineering question that requires explanation, tradeoff analysis, or system reasoning.

## Expected Answer

Describe the core points a strong candidate should cover, including definitions, mechanics, and practical implications.

## Deep Dive

Expand into the details that distinguish a good answer from a senior or staff-level answer.

## Follow-up Questions

- Ask follow-ups that explore scale, failure, security, reliability, ownership, or cost.

## Common Mistakes

- Identify weak answers, shallow assumptions, and missing production considerations.

## Staff Engineer Perspective

Explain how a staff-level engineer frames the problem across teams, constraints, risk, and long-term maintainability.
