# Engineering story title

## Context

Describe the system, team, business pressure, and technical environment before the problem appeared.

## Problem

Explain the concrete engineering problem and why it mattered.

## Symptoms

List the observable signals: user impact, alerts, metrics, logs, deployment changes, or team reports.

## Investigation

Describe the reasoning path, evidence gathered, hypotheses considered, and experiments performed.

## Root Cause

Explain the underlying technical and organizational cause without blaming individuals.

## Resolution

Describe the fix, mitigation, rollback, redesign, or process change that resolved the issue.

## Lessons Learned

- Capture durable lessons about systems, ownership, operations, reliability, security, or communication.

## Interview Takeaway

Explain how the story can be used to demonstrate practical judgment in an interview.
